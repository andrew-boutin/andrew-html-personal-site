Geo Wars

  • Geo Wars Dev Info - A page on my site for info on the technology, how it was made, my role, etc.

    Game Overview:
    Geo Wars is a 2D single player side-scroller platform shooter. The player has control over their character (left, right, and jump) and has access to four different weapons: pistol, shotgun, and two automatic weapons. Currently there is only one level consisting of platforms, spikes, red grunts, and a ninja warrior at the end. The grunts start moving towards the player when within a certain distance. The boss can jump, has a sword, and chases after the player when within a certain distance. If the player hits the spikes or any of the enemies they die and start back at the beginning of the level. The player gets three lives and in order to beat the level they have to kill the boss. The object is to clear the level as many times as possible with only the three lives. Enemies get more health and are faster each level.

    How to Play:
    I'm in the process of contacting the game owner to figure out why the game isn't playable at the moment. The game is hosted on Kongregate [Play GeoWars] and requires WebPlayer to be installed. WebPlayer allows games developed using Unity to be played in web browsers. If you have a Kongregate account then you can get your high score saved on the site.

Labyrinth Crawl

  • Labyrinth Crawl Dev Info - A page on my site for info on the technology, how it was made, etc.

    Game Overview:
    Labyrinth Crawl is a terminal based strategy game, and the object is to make it through 10 levels to escape. Each level is a randomly generated maze with many dead ends. Also, each level is littered with enemies that are randomly placed throughout the maze. They don't move but can block paths. The player can attack the enemies at the risk of losing health to continue down the path. Each level of the labyrinth has more enemies than the previous one. Another twist is the player only sees parts of the map that they have traversed so far. This forces the player to choose which enemies to battle to open up certain paths. The player starts off with full health on each each level of the maze. This game forces the player to use some strategy combined with a bit of luck to make it out alive.

    How to Play:
    Feel free to download Labyrinth Crawl and play the game.

Current Projects

Crazy Canal Tower Defense

  • Game Overview:
    This game is a tower defense game that's still in progress. The player has to create defense towers along a canal to protect their base from the enemies who are trying to destroy it. The enemies drive boats down the canal and attempt to reach the base to do damage to it. Currently, there is 1 map, 3 different basic tower types, and an assortment of enemy types.

    How to Play:
    The plan is to host this on Kongregate in the near future.

Pirates

  • Note: I am no longer contributing to this project, and the game is still in production.

    Pirates Dev Info - A page on my site for info on the technology, how it was being made, my role, etc.

    Game Overview:
    Pirates is a 3D combat based game that is currently in the Alpha Stage. It's an on-line, multi-player PvP game with melee and ranged combat. There are many modes including: death-match, capture the flag, king of the hill, etc. These modes can be free-for-all or team based. Games will support up to 64 players - 32 per team. The game maps consist of islands, ruins, etc., and some modes are played on the open sea where teams battle each other from their ships.
    Progress:
    Currently, the game has basic maps, an animated moveable player, a basic combat system, supports multiple clients on the servers, and other basic features. More weapons, players, ships, and map objects are being created. More advanced movement and combat features are also being added. Check out the Main Page and IndieDB Site for the game. There are also blogs, images, etc. all over the internet - like this Preview Article. If you want to search for other info/media on the game use Miriscus and Pirates. The plan for the near future is to go for Steam Greenlight for initial crowd funding.
